Our Business

Beerenstank is a global provider of UN T75 cryogenic tanks

The most efficient and optimal tank containers with the longest holding time on the market available for the transport and storage of cryogenic products. Beerenstank currently has 2000 brand new T75 cryogenic tanks available for lease. Regardless of your specific storage specifications or distinct commercial purposes, Beerenstank tanks offer a suite of innovative design elements to meet virtually any specified need, and our overall maintenance costs are among the lowest.

We are truly international company, centrally located within the Dubai Multi Commodities Centre (DMCC) at the heart of Dubai’s Jumeirah Lakes Towers district. The largest free trade zone in the world, the DMCC was twice crowned “Global Free Zone of the Year” by the Financial Times fDi Magazine. Featuring a vibrant business community, state-of-the-art internal infrastructure and rapid access to international air, sea and ground transportation trade routes, the DMCC is the perfect base of operations for Beerenstank’s global headquarters and worldwide distribution hub.
